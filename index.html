About

Chris Craigen is a talented Bedford-based singer and pianist, specialising in delivering exceptional live music for weddings, private events, and corporate functions. Whether it's romantic piano melodies for a wedding ceremony or captivating vocals for an evening reception, Chris crafts the perfect soundtrack to elevate any occasion.

With extensive experience performing on international stages and for local Bedfordshire audiences, Chris brings versatility, professionalism, and a personal touch to every performance. If you're planning an intimate gathering or a large-scale event, Chris Craigen's passion for music ensures a memorable experience for you and your guests.
